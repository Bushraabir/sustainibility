Global Warming and Climate Change

Climate change is caused by human activities like burning fossil fuels and deforestation, leading to increased greenhouse gases in the atmosphere.

Deforestation

Massive deforestation contributes to biodiversity loss and climate change. Countries like Brazil and Indonesia are facing significant deforestation challenges.
